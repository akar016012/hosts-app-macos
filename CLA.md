## How to sign

You only need to do this once. Either:

1. **Automated (preferred):** the CLA bot comments on your first pull request. To
   sign, reply on the PR with exactly:

   > I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

   Your agreement is recorded in `signatures/version1/cla.json` against your GitHub
   account, and the PR's CLA check turns green. **Or**
2. **Manual:** add a line to [`CONTRIBUTORS`](CONTRIBUTORS) in your pull request:

   ```
   Full Name <email>  —  I have read and agree to the Hosts CLA (CLA.md).
   ```

Submitting a Contribution after agreeing constitutes acceptance of this CLA for
that and all future Contributions to the project.
